Instructions:

Desktop:

For Desktop you can use arrows in your keyboard to sketch.

Touch screen and mobile:

If you have touch screen device or using a mobile device, you can use those knobs to sketch.

  • Turn the left knob to sketch horizontally.
  • Turn the right knob to draw vertically.
