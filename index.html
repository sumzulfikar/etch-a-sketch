Your imagination, pixelated.

Keyboard Controls:

- Up/Down arrows draw vertical lines.

- Left/Right arrows draw horizontal lines.

Mouse/Touch screen controls:

Click or touch to drag the knobs.

- The right knob draws vertical lines.

- The left knob draws horizontal lines.

* Don't forget to save the sketch by pressing save button on top!

Pro-tip: Try to move knobs at the same time to create diagonal lines!
