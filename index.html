Get Your Sketch On!

Sketch and Shake

Ready to unleash your inner artist? This digital canvas is ready for your masterpiece (or masterpiece-in-progress). Here's how to make your magic happen:

Desktop Darlings:

Feeling classic? Use those trusty arrow keys to navigate your way to sketch glory! Up and down for horizontal lines, left and right for vertical. Easy as pie (or maybe a tasty doodle?)

Mouseketeers:

Love a good point-and-click? Grab those knobs on the side and drag them around to create your lines! Left knob controls vertical movement, right knob for horizontal.

Touchscreen Titans::

Got a fancy touch screen device? No problem! Swipe up and down on the left knob for vertical lines, and left and right on the right knob for horizontal lines. Simple as that!
